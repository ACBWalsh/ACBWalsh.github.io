Angela Walsh (she/her) is currently a student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln majoring in Emerging Media Arts with an emphasis in Cinematic Arts and Virtual Production. She is exploring her options for grad school. Basically she's a film kid with an emphasis on animation and VFX.

When she's not working on projects, Angela like to write, listen to music, and consume media in all forms - books, YouTube, TV shows, movies, podcasts, etc.
